UNIT SET AT ADDRESS ZERO  SO SOME OF THE COMMANDS HAVE A LEADING ZERO
SYSTEM IS SET AT 4000 STEPS PER REV OF THE MOTOR.

A10  sets acceleration to 10 revs/second squared
V10 sets velocity to 10 revs per second
D2000  sets distance to 2000 steps
G executes the move

GH-2  GO HOME

K  KILL MOTOR

H+  SETS CW  MOTION
H- SETS CCW MOTION

0LD0 ENABLES THE HOME LIMITS
0LD3 DISABLES THE HOME LIMITS

MC  SETS ROTOR ON ALL OF TIME
MN  SETS ROTOR BACK TO NORMAL
0Z   RESETS SYSTEM
